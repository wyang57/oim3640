<!-- ## My Project Proposal -->

<!-- What I'm building:   -->
I am building a simple text analysis tool that analyzes a section of my own writing (my utopia project). The program will load the text from a file, process it, and find basic patterns such as word frequency and vocabulary usage.

<!-- Why I chose this:   -->
I chose my own writing because it’s personal, easy to access, and meaningful to analyze. It also avoids copyright issues and lets me see patterns in my writing style as I continue developing my utopia book idea.

<!-- Core features:   -->
- Load text from a .txt file  
- Count total words and unique words  
- Identify the most common words  
- Clean the text (lowercase, remove punctuation, remove stop words)  
- Create at least one visualization (bar chart of top words)

<!-- What I don't know yet:   -->
- How to structure the text‑cleaning pipeline  
- How to remove stop words effectively  
- What visualization will be most useful  
- Whether I want to compare different sections of my writing later on
